About Us

Welcome to MAD - Meal At Doorstep

At MAD, we are passionate about bringing the world's best cuisines straight to your doorstep. Whether you're craving traditional dishes from India or exotic flavors from Japan, we have a wide variety of food options to satisfy every palate.

Our mission is to provide a convenient, affordable, and delightful food ordering experience, ensuring that you can enjoy authentic meals from different cultures, all in one place. We work closely with local chefs and global food artisans to bring high-quality, freshly prepared meals right to your home.

Our Values:

  • Quality: We use only the finest ingredients to create dishes that are full of flavor and authentic taste.
  • Variety: Our menu spans across multiple cuisines, offering a diverse range of options from around the world.
  • Convenience: Ordering your favorite dishes is just a click away, and we ensure fast delivery to your door.
  • Customer Satisfaction: We aim to provide an excellent service and a memorable dining experience with every order.
